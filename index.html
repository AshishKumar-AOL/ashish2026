Sri Sri Ravi Shankar Ji is a globally revered humanitarian, spiritual leader, and founder of the Art of Living Foundation — one of the world's largest volunteer-based organizations, active in over 180 countries. His teachings on breath, meditation, and human values have brought peace and joy to over 500 million people worldwide.
